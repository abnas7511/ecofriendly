Trash Can Relay

You will need
  • Five trash cans
How to
  1. Divide the kids into two teams.
  2. Arrange the five trash cans to create an obstacle course. The first player of each team has to run the course, weaving in and out the garbage cans.
  3. When the player reaches the last can, he has to turn around and twist back to the team.

Why Learn About the Environment?

Understanding the environment is crucial for a sustainable future. By learning about the environment from an early age, children can develop a sense of responsibility towards nature and become future stewards of the planet.

What You Can Explore

  • Games: Engage in interactive games that teach important environmental concepts while having fun.
  • Activities: Participate in hands-on activities that inspire creativity and promote eco-friendly practices.
  • Resources: Access a wide range of educational materials, including articles, videos, and downloadable guides.

Our Mission

At the Environmental Education Portal, our mission is to empower children with knowledge and skills to make a positive impact on the environment. We believe that by educating the younger generation, we can create a more sustainable future for all.

Join Us on the Journey

We invite you to join us on this exciting journey of environmental education. Together, we can inspire children to become environmentally conscious individuals and make a difference in the world.
